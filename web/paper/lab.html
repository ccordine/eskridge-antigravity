14) Predictions and Failure Modes

In-model predictions

  • Hover possible as C -> 0.
  • Repulsive regime possible for C < 0.
  • Lock stability depends on Q, drift, and actuator limits.
  • Controller/PLL conflict can create oscillatory behavior.
  • Large coupling shifts require rate limits to avoid violent acceleration.

Failure modes

  • Loss of lock: abrupt gravity return.
  • Thermal drift: natural frequency shifts out of lock window.
  • Phase slip: unexpected sign flips in coupling.
  • Energy exhaustion: coupling decays toward default gravity.
