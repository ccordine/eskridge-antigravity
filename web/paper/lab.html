Flight Guide

  1. Choose a scenario and warp drive: use free_play with standard anti-gravity (PLL) for the default sandbox launch.
  2. Pick your ship and map: choose hull type, set craft scale, and set map mode to local (flat) or planetary (globe).
  3. Start the session: click Start Session. Keep Start On Ground enabled for easier takeoff.
  4. Apply lift control: increase amplitude with D and adjust phase bias with W/S until weight ratio nears hover.
  5. Shape the oscillator: use Tune ω0, Q, and Pump β to retune resonance, change losses, and alter spin-up authority.
  6. Use plasma only in air: raise Plasma Sheath to trade extra electrical draw for modest drag reduction and flow-control authority inside an atmosphere.
  7. Steer direction: use Q/E to pick a heading around the planet and I/K to tilt the warp axis away from local vertical.
  8. Stabilize flight: toggle Auto Trim Assist and use preset buttons (Hover, Ascend, Descend) as a baseline.
  9. Watch telemetry: track Weight xg, Lock, Warp X/Y/Z, and Runaway while flying.
  10. Recover quickly: press Space for lock assist, P to pause, and R to reset.
