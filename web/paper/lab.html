Flight Lab Game

Playable fixed-step simulation lab. Inputs only drive coupler targets and lock behavior; craft motion remains fully physical. The default scenario is free_play for manual PLL/coupler piloting with a saucer hull and EM warp-axis steering.

ready

Controls: keyboard A/D amp, W/S phase, Q/E warp yaw, I/K warp pitch, Space lock assist, R reset.

No direct physics overrides are used. UI commands only feed control inputs that the simulation integrates.
