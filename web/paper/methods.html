Experimental Separation Tests

10) Experimental Philosophy

If someone claims gravity modification, most apparent effects are likely artifacts:

  • Ion wind / corona discharge
  • Thermal convection
  • Vibration coupling
  • Magnetic coupling to fixtures
  • Electrostatic attraction

So the key discriminator is whether the effect survives without momentum exchange with the environment.

10.1) Primary Discriminator

  • Effect persists in vacuum or sealed still air.
  • Effect persists at rest on a scale.
  • Effect cannot be explained by airflow or moving-field drag.

10.2) Signature Observation (if real)

Static weight change at rest. Not just slower fall, odd motion, or damping. A true coupling shift changes scale reading without requiring motion.

11) Simulation Program

  • Minimal 1D: position/velocity, raw gravity, coupler, energy store, optional controller.
  • Full 3D: quaternion attitude, optional directional coupling, drag, multi-body gravity, replay logs.
  • No-hacks contract: no direct altitude set, no velocity clamp, no teleport state edits.

12) Tesla Resonance Connection (conceptual)

Use resonance principles, not mythology: high-Q systems amplify targeted modes but require tight control. Off-resonance drive turns into heat/noise, not useful coupling.

13) Casimir / Negative Energy Position

Casimir effects motivate "configuration-dependent force" thinking, but are not treated as a direct scalable anti-gravity mechanism in this framework.
