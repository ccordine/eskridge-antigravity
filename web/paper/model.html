Resonator, Lock, and Energy

7) Energy Bookkeeping: No Free Lunch

Even in an example universe, stable operating modes require an energy pathway:

  • Drive power feeds the resonator.
  • Losses occur through damping, heat, radiation, or field leakage.
  • If stored energy depletes, coupling collapses.

The simulator logs drive power, stored energy, achieved coupling, and failure events such as lock loss and drift.

8) What This Model Explains Well

  • Hover without thrust: reduce coupling, reduce downward acceleration.
  • "Falls forward" framing: shape coupling gradients to bias effective falling direction.
  • Violent failure: lock snapback causes abrupt acceleration change and structural stress.

9) What This Model Does Not Explain (and must not pretend to)

  • Maglev proves antigravity: false. Maglev proves EM can oppose gravity, not that gravity is EM.
  • Frequency notching Earth gravity: in standard physics near-Earth gravity is effectively DC, not a carrier wave.

The example-universe assumption is a tunable coupling phase in craft-state space, not a gravity broadcast frequency.
