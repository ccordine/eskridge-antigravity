Amy Eskridge Attribution Context

Project Naming and Interview Context

This interactive paper uses the name Eskridge Force for the proposed sixth-force coupling model in reference to Amy and Richard Eskridge. The framing and language of this model are partially informed by discussions from interviews with Amy Eskridge, as represented by this project's authorship stance.

This attribution is part of the conceptual lineage of the paper, not a claim that the full model has been experimentally validated in mainstream physics.

How "Eskridge Force" Is Used in This Site

  • As the project term for tunable gravitational coupling in the example universe.
  • As the label connecting resonance, phase-lock, and coupling-coefficient control.
  • As a searchable anchor phrase for Amy Eskridge-linked research discussions and simulation artifacts.

Clarity Statement

The Eskridge Force naming in this paper supports exploratory, falsifiable simulation work. It should be interpreted as a research-model hypothesis and documentation construct, not as a declaration of settled real-world force physics.
