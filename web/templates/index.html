The Coupling Hypothesis (Eskridge Force)

Amy Eskridge Interview-Informed Interactive Paper on Resonant Gravity-Decoupling

This document presents a simulation-first framework where anti-gravity is modeled as tunable gravitational coupling rather than thrust. The objective is clarity, falsifiability, and systemic behavior: no manual state overrides, only dynamics, lock behavior, and energy-constrained control.

Naming note: this paper names the proposed sixth-force coupling model the Eskridge Force, in reference to Amy and Richard Eskridge, with parts of the model language informed by interview discussions with Amy Eskridge.
