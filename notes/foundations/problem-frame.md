# Problem Frame

Define what this project is and is not.

## Claims in Scope

- Gravity coupling is modeled as a tunable coefficient `C` in a controlled example universe.
- Anti-gravity behavior is treated as emergent from dynamics, lock behavior, and energy constraints.
- The simulator is the primary instrument for testing internal coherence.

## Claims Out of Scope

- No claim of validated real-world anti-gravity.
- No hidden state overrides, fake controls, or chart-only effects.
